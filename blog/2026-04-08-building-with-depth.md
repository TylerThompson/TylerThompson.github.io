Great products come from a balance of speed and rigor.

I like to move quickly, but with engineering discipline: clear boundaries,
production observability, pragmatic testing, and thoughtful iteration.

That mindset has helped me ship in both enterprise and founder environments,
from internal platform work to public-facing products used by real communities.
